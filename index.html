STEEL GYM & FITNESS

Established in 1992, Steel Gym & fitness is the Longest Running Gym in the County! It is currently owned and operated since 2005 by Phil DeCresto, former Mr Universe competitor, equipment specialist, bodybuilding historian, and holder of 9 personal training certifications including NASM, and Resistance Training Specialist Levels 1 & 2. This summer marks his 28th year in business!
